JetBrains IntelliJ Platform Icons
==================================

License text: Apache-2.0.txt in this metadata folder, also available at https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

Each embedded SVG under actionicons/ and the derived single-color SVG under actionsymbols/ preserves the JetBrains copyright and Apache 2.0 license header from the original source file.
The actionsymbols files are color-normalized derivatives of the same Apache-licensed SVGs for Logitech/Loupedeck action symbol requirements.
